Holiday Tip:

After carving our your pumpkins into a spooky design, keep the pumpkin seeds to create a snack. All you have to do is rinse them, boil them in salted water (this way the salt gets on the inside and outside of the seeds), coat them in butter and then roast them in the oven.

Enjoy!
