Banana Buttermilk Pancakes

These Banana Buttermilk Pancakes are a twist on the All Recipes Buttermilk Pancakes Recipe. This version teaches you how to make your own buttermilk and adds banana for some more flavor. Prep Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients:

  • scant cup(s) of milk
  • tbsp(s) of vinegar
  • cup(s) of flour
  • tbsp(s) of white sugar
  • tsp(s) of baking powder
  • tsp(s) of baking soda
  • tsp(s) of salt
  • cup(s) of butter
  • banana
  • tsp(s) of vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Use a measuring cup to add milk and vinegar together and let sit for eight minutes - stir every now and then to make buttermilk
  2. During this time, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda and salt together in a large bowl
  3. Use a smaller bowl to heat butter in microwave for 30 seconds
  4. After the buttermilk is ready, mix it together with the butter
  5. Mash up the banana and add it to the buttermilk mixture
  6. Add the liquid mixture into the big bowl with solid ingredients and mix together until the batter is slightly lumpy
  7. Add vanilla extract to the mixture
  8. The pancake mixture is now ready to be cooked

Portion Size:

*This portion size makes about - pancakes depending on their size*

Pro Tip

If you don't have vinegar at home for the buttermilk, you can always use lemon juice. And if you want some more or alternate flavors, you can add fruits and even some cinnamon into the mixture.

Enjoy!
