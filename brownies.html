Mint Chip Brownies

This recipe is a take on Betty Crocker’s Classic Fudge Brownie Recipe that just adds a little more flavor. The fun things about this recipe is you can really edit it as much as you like and there’s no way to really mess up. If you see any ingredients you don't own, just click the plus and it will be added to the cart. Click again to remove. Note that this will purchase a general amount/container of the product and not just the amount set in the ingredients list.

Prep Time: 15-25 minutes

Ingredients:

  • box mix(es) of brownies
  • cup(s) of mint chips or addition of your choice

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to the set time on the brownie mix
  2. Freeze mint chips for about 10 minutes
  3. Follow the instructions for cooking the brownies
  4. Add the mint chips into the batter and make sure to mix so that they are distributed evenly
  5. Put the batter in a pan and cook in oven
  6. Sprinkle some mint chips on top and let cool after pulling out of oven

Portion Size:

*This portion size makes the number of servings on the box (x)*

Pro Tip

This recipe is all about personal taste and having fun. Feel free to replace the mint chips in this recipe with anything from strawberries to m＆ms.
