Bizcochitos

Bizcochitos are small Mexican cookies flavoured with cinnamon and sugar. They can often be found around the winter holidays in fun little shapes. Our unique recipe utilizes grapefruit juice in order for the cookies to cook better. In this recipe you will learn how to make these cookies from scratch! Prep Time: 15-25 minutes

Ingredients:

  • tbsps of crisco
  • + cups of sugar
  • + tsps of cinnamon powder
  • egg(s)
  • tbsp(s) of baking powder
  • cups of flour
  • cup(s) of grapefruit juice

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350Â°F
  2. Blend together crisco, cup(s) of sugar and tsp(s) of cinnamon in a large bowl with a mixer for about 40 seconds
  3. Add flour, eggs, baking powder and grapefruit juice and blend for 1-2 minutes
  4. Begin kneading the dough together until it takes on a medium soft consistency like the picture below
  5. Put dough into a cookie press and press the cookies onto a pan
  6. Bake cookies for 10-12 minutes
  7. During this duration, mix together cup(s) of sugar and tsp(s) of cinnamon together in a bowl
  8. Let cool for about 3 minutes
  9. As soon as possible, takes the cookies off of the cookie sheet and coat them in the mixture of cinnamon and sugar
  10. Let any excess spice fall off the cookie and place it on a plate or wherever you plan on storing them

Portion Size:

*This portion size makes about cookies*
