Arroz Con Leche

Arroz Con Leche is a classic Mexican dish that is often served as a rice pudding. This take on the recipe allows you to incorporate the same flavors into rice that has already been cooked and can just be reheated in the microwave. This meal is often served as a sweet breakfast treat or as dessert. If you see any ingredients you don't own, just click the plus and it will be added to the cart. Click again to remove. Note that this will purchase a general amount/container of the product and not just the amount set in the ingredients list.

Prep Time: 3 minutes

Ingredients:

  • ~ cups of pre-cooked rice
  • ~ cup(s) of milk (mostly depends on personal preference)
  • spoonful(s) of white sugar
  • dashes of cinnamon
  • handful(s) of raisins (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Put the rice in a bowl and re-heat it for 1 minute in microwave
  2. Add as much milk as desired
  3. Mix in sugar
  4. Add dashes of cinnamon on top
  5. If desired, add raisins

Portion Size:

*This portion size makes about cups of rice*

Pro Tip

If you want this recipe to better mimic the traditional rice pudding. Use a rice cooker to cook the rice with the milk, cinnamon and sugar inside. That way the flavors will be baked into the rice for richer flavors.

Enjoy!
